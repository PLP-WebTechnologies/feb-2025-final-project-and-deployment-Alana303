Learning Web Design & Building a Personal Website

Published on April 13, 2025 by Jeff Amayo

Why Learn Web Design?

Web design is the foundation of a compelling online presence. Understanding design principles empowers you to build user-friendly, accessible, and aesthetically pleasing websites that captivate visitors.

Steps to Build Your Personal Website

  • Start with HTML & CSS to create structure and style
  • Learn responsive design to support multiple devices
  • Incorporate JavaScript to add interactivity
  • Host your site and create a custom domain

Whether you're a student, freelancer, or job-seeker, a personal website serves as your digital resume, showcasing your skills, projects, and professional story in a unique way.
