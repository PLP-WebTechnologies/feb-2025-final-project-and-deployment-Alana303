Building a Personal Website

Published on April 13, 2025 by Jeff Amayo

Why Build a Personal Website?

A personal website acts as your digital identity. It allows you to showcase your achievements, share your experiences, and position yourself professionally in the tech world or any industry.

Essential Elements of a Strong Personal Website

  • Clear introduction with a professional photo
  • Portfolio of your projects and skills
  • Interactive contact form or email link
  • Links to social profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.)
  • Responsive layout that looks good on all devices

Whether you're job hunting, freelancing, or simply sharing your journey, a personal website is a powerful tool to tell your story, establish credibility, and grow your network.
